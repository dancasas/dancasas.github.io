SMPLitex Dataset

SMPLitex enables the synthesis of textures for SMPL model. For those interested in just accessing to a large number of textures, we have created a curated dataset of SMPL textures generated with SMPLitex. You can donwload the full dataset here or browse and download individual textures below.

  • SMPLitex-textures.zip



Please note that is dataset is under construction and growing. Feel free to send your best SMPLitex samples or any SMPL textures and we will add it into the dataset.
